What is Vehicle Platooning?

Platooning is technology that controls the speed and distance of vehicles between other vehicles by using vehicle to vehicle communication (V2V) and sensors such as cameras and GPS.

Why use Platooning?

Platooning aims to link vehicles to one another thereby reducing the risks of crashes and making the road more efficient by keeping a constant distance between each vehicle.
